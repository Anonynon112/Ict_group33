About

Group 33


We’re Group 33 — a group of students with a shared passion for exploring the world and building things that matter. As part of our web development project, we decided to create a travel website
that captures the spirit of adventure and discovery. This site is our way of combining creativity with code. From dreamy destinations to useful travel tips, we wanted to design a space that inspires
and informs. Along the way, we’ve learned how to turn ideas into webpages, and how to work together like a real team. Whether you’re planning your next trip or just daydreaming about
far-off places we hope our site gets you one step closer to your next adventure. Thanks for traveling with us. 🌍✈️
